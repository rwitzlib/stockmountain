# Support US Equities and ETFs in V1

StockMountain supports US equities and ETFs in the first version. Options, crypto, forex, futures, short selling, and other asset classes are out of scope for V1.

**Consequences**

Market data ingestion, Universes, Strategy evaluation, Paper Bots, and Backtest Runs can assume stock-like symbols, bar data, long-only Trades, and US market session behavior in the first version.
